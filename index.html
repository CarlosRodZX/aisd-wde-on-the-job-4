About Us

The Devs is a passionate and dynamic group of musicians dedicated to creating and performing unforgettable music. Our journey began in 2024, and since then, we have been committed to sharing our love for music with audiences around the world.

Our band members include:

  • John: Guitar/Vocals
  • Ryan: Bass
  • Lindsay: Keys
  • Logan: Drums

With a diverse range of influences and a unique sound, we strive to push the boundaries of our genre and deliver performances that are both energetic and heartfelt. Whether we're in the studio or on stage, our goal is to connect with our fans and create music that resonates with them.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to sharing many more moments and memories with you.
